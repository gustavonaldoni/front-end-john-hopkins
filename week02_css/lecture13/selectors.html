Do you know how to cook rice?

How to cook rice properly:
  1. Rinse the rice.
  2. Bring the water to a boil.
  3. Get a steady simmer.
  4. Don't take off the lid.
  5. Check at 18 minutes.
    1. If the rice is great.
    2. If the rice is not dust.
  6. Leave it alone.
  7. Fluff it up and eat it!
